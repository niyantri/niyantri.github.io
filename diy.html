Holiday DIY!

Welcome. My holiday DIY's are easy to do and will be super fun and entertaining! Do you love shamrock slurpees or christmas cookies or maybe even valentine heart cake pops!

Valentine Heart Cakepops are the easiest to make. Simply start of with any batter of your choice, I will use red velvet for its color. Then get a cookie cutter in the shape of a heart or you can make one from plastic. Then cute some of the baked cake make sure to get it from the inside so it will be thick, moist, and fluffy. Then cool the cake pop and then take greek yogurt and add 3/4 cups of sugar. Then dip into food coloring. Decorations can be made after! You can share this with anyone and it will be a very cute gift!

Mothers Day Nutella Crepes are the most fun to make and will be super easy. Start of with a batter of flour, baking soda and eggs. Then put your batter on a non-stick pan. Keep 40 seconds on each side. After that you have to spread nutella on your crepe then add any berries and whipped crean. A super yummy but simple Mothers day breakfast!

Trail Mix easter Egg- Start with peanuts and salt them, next add spring edition m&m's. After that throw in a pack of raisins and some chocolate chips. These can be perfect for fun spring hikes with your fam and friends!

Peppermint Hot Chocolate are the best hot chocolate's ever. First get a packet of hot chocolate mix then add water or milk then add 1 teaspoon of peppermint extract. Mix well then add any whipped cream, peppermint preferably! You can also crush peppermint or candy canes on the top.!
